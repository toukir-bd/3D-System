ABOUT US

Who We Are

3D Systems Contractors Limited is a leading procurement and contracting company, established in 2014 and headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Our company specializes in the supply of a wide range of high-quality products and equipment to government institutions, security agencies, and private sector clients. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation for our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our expertise spans various industries, including security, agriculture, IT, transportation, healthcare, and general office supplies. With a decade of experience, we continue to enhance our service offerings to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
